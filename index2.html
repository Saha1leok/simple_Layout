Tribute to Darkhan Juzz

About Darkhan Juzz

Darkhan Juzz (1990–2023) was a Kazakh indie-pop singer, songwriter, and producer. Born in Almaty, he became one of the most beloved young artists in Kazakhstan, known for blending modern sounds with deep lyrics. His hit "Sheker" made him famous, and after his tragic passing in 2023, his album "aūe" received the BEU Award for Best Album.

Life & Legacy

He left behind two children and a musical legacy that continues to inspire fans.

Important Events

  1. 1990 – Born in Almaty, Kazakhstan.
  2. Released first notable hit “Sheker”.
  3. Became popular on streaming platforms with unique indie-pop style.
  4. 2023 – Passed away in Phuket, Thailand.
  5. 2023 – Posthumous album "aūe" released and awarded Best Album.

Key Contributions

  • Blending indie and pop with Kazakh cultural identity.
  • Inspiring young musicians with fresh sound and lyrics.
  • Leaving a strong legacy in Kazakh modern music.
